Santorini Greek Shrimp

source:allrecipes.com Photo by Jonboy924

Description

This Greek Dish hails from Santorini and is an easy dish to make; that can also give you the feel of being transported to a Greek island. This dish is also easy to prepare ahead of time and best served over rice

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • 2 pints grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 (750 milliliter) bottle dry white wine
  • 2 pounds peeled and deveined medium shrimp
  • 1(4ounce) container crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 lemons, halved
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in garlic, chopped parsley, tomatoes, and wine.
  2. Bring to a simmer; reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has slightly thickened.
  3. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C)
  4. Stir shrimp into tomato sauce and cook for 2 min; remove from heat
  5. Pour Shrimp mixture into a shallow baking dish; sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until feta has softened and shrimp are no longer translucent, 45 min to 1hr
  7. Squeeze lemon halves over shrimp and garnish with parsley sprigs to serve
